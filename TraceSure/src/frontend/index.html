TraceSure

GxP Sample Tracking Web App

The GxP Sample Tracking Web App is a lightweight internal system designed to manage laboratory samples throughout their lifecycle. It brings together secure user authentication, role based access control, audit trails, electronic signatures, and controlled status transitions to support compliant operations. Built to demonstrate a complete CSV lifecycle, the application showcases end to end validation practices — from documentation and testing to deviation management and CAPA.

How TraceSure benefits you, your business and your employees.

End to End Compliance Built In

The system enforces controlled status transitions, electronic signatures, audit trails, and role based permissions — giving teams a compliant workflow without needing external tools or manual oversight.

Clear, Traceable Sample Lifecycle Management

Every sample is tracked from creation to closure with full visibility. Users can see who did what, when, and why, reducing errors and strengthening data integrity.

Secure Access and Segregation of Duties

With authentication, JWT tokens, and Admin controlled RBAC, the app ensures that technicians, supervisors, QA, and managers each operate within their authorized boundaries, preventing unauthorized actions.

Demonstrates a Complete CSV Lifecycle

The app isn't just functional — it's a teaching and validation tool. It showcases documentation, testing, deviation handling, and CAPA workflows, making it ideal for training, audits, and internal validation exercises.
